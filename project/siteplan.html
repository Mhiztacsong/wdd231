Site Name

Proposed Site Name: Mental Health and Wellness Resource

Reason for Selection: Mental health is an important issue, and providing a platform for education and support can help reduce stigma and promote well-being

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information, resources, and support for mental health awareness and self-care strategies. it could include articles, guided meditations and other relevant mental health informations

Scenarios

  • How can i manage my anxiety during stressful situations?
  • Are there any mindfulness exercise i can try at home?
  • How can i find a licensed therapist near me?
  • What type of therapy are available for depression?
  • What signs should i look for if someone needs professional help?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4caf50(for header background)

Secondary Color: #2ecc71 (Green for buttons and call-to-action elements)

Background Colors: #eef3ef and #333 (Light green for sections background and dark red for footer background)

Typography

Font 1: helvetica - Used for Headings

Font 2: cambria - Used for paragraph text
